A word on the prices...

I took the time to find the lowest cost parts. I search online bought from friends and asked around. I don’t consider the prices I paid to be extremely below average but I did get a deal on most of the parts.

I think anyone who spends the time to look for parts like I did can find them at the price I paid or close to it.
